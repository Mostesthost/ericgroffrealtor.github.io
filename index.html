Buying a Home in Southwest Ohio

Whether you're a first-time buyer or looking to move up, I'll guide you every step of the way with patience and expertise.

The Home Buying Process – Step by Step

  1. Get Pre-Approved – Know what you can afford and show sellers you're serious.
  2. Define Your Needs – Location, size, schools, commute – I'll help you clarify priorities.
  3. House Hunting – I'll search the market and show you homes that match your goals.
  4. Make an Offer – I'll negotiate on your behalf to get the best price and terms.
  5. Home Inspection & Appraisal – Protect your investment with thorough due diligence.
  6. Closing – I'll be with you until the keys are in your hand.

First-Time Buyer Tips from Eric

  • Take advantage of Ohio’s down payment assistance programs (many can be combined).
  • Don’t rush – the right home is worth waiting for.
  • Factor in future costs: taxes, insurance, maintenance, and utilities.
  • I’ll help you understand your credit score and how to improve it if needed.
  • Consider the “hidden” costs of homeownership – I’ll walk you through them honestly.
